# Brand Guidelines: Quiet Reconnection

## Keywords

- Safety
- Silence
- Renewal
- Dignity

## Color Palette

- **Primary:** Soft Blues (e.g., #AEC6CF, #B0C4DE)
- **Secondary:** Warm Creams (e.g., #F5F5DC, #FFFDD0)
- **Accent:** Muted Greens (e.g., #B2D8B2, #C9DDC9)

## Voice

**The Wise Friend:** Empathetic but authoritative. Never judgmental.

- **Tone:** Calm, reassuring, and understanding.
- **Language:** Simple, direct, and free of jargon. Use gentle and encouraging words.
- **Perspective:** Speak as a peer who has gone through a similar experience and has found a path to healing and renewal.
